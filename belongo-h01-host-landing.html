Pricing & the fine print

No surprises for you, either

The same honesty we give guests, we give hosts. Here’s exactly what it costs and how it works.

What does it cost?

A flat commission from 12% of each booking — all-in. No listing fee, no monthly fee, no charge for the dashboard or AI pricing.

When do I get paid?

Weekly, every Monday, to your local bank in your currency. Guest part-payments are passed through on the same schedule.

Who handles cancellations & refunds?

We do — guests see a transparent refund timeline, and your payout is reconciled automatically. You’re never chasing money.

Can I keep my other listings elsewhere?

Yes. No exclusivity, no lock-in. Sync your calendar to avoid double-bookings and cancel whenever you like.

Do I control my pricing?

Always. AI suggests; you set floors, ceilings and whether to auto-apply. It assists — it never takes the wheel.

What about long-stay tenants & leases?

Belongo handles both nightly stays and longer leases — track tenants, contracts and renewals in the same dashboard.
